Our Story

Born in Portland in 2019, Meadow & Stone started with a simple mission: to celebrate the incredible artisans and makers in our community.

We carefully curate every piece—from hand-thrown pottery to soy-wax candles—ensuring that each item tells a story. Our physical shop on NW 23rd Avenue has become a gathering place for lovers of handcrafted goods, and we're thrilled to bring our selection online to reach even more homes.

Every purchase supports local makers and sustainable practices. When you shop with us, you're investing in artistry and community.
