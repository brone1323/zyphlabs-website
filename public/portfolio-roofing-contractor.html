How a Roof Gets Done

From first call to final walkthrough — here's exactly what happens.

Day 1

Free Inspection & Estimate

We come out, inspect with drone footage, and hand you a written estimate on the spot. No pressure, no sales games.

Day 2-3

Material Selection

Pick your shingles, metal panels, or tile from our showroom samples. We'll match your home's style and your budget.

Day 7

Permits & Scheduling

We pull all permits and lock in your install date. Materials are ordered and staged.

Day 10-14

Installation

Our crew arrives at 7am sharp. Full tear-off, inspection, underlayment, and new roof — typically complete in 1-3 days.
