Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What do I need to bring to my appointment? +

Please bring a valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, etc.) and the documents you need notarized. For some services like power of attorney, we may provide sample documents for you to review.

Do I need an appointment, or can I just walk in? +

Both! You can schedule an appointment online for convenience, or simply walk in. Walk-ins are always welcome, though appointment times may have shorter wait times, especially during busy hours.

How long does it take? +

Most notary services take 5-15 minutes. Document preparation services typically take 15-45 minutes depending on complexity. Real estate closings may take 30-60 minutes. We'll give you an estimate when you call or arrive.

Do you offer mobile notary services? +

Yes! We offer mobile notary services if you can't come to our offices. Travel fees apply based on distance. Call either location to schedule a mobile notary appointment.

What forms of payment do you accept? +

We accept cash, credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, and digital payment options like Venmo and PayPal for your convenience.

Can you help with documents in other languages? +

We can notarize documents in other languages. However, for document preparation services, we primarily work in English. For complex multilingual documents, we can refer you to specialized services.
