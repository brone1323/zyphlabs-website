The Challenge

VELA was bleeding customers

VELA's Shopify store was driving traffic, but converting at just 1.8%. Their design was outdated, mobile experience was poor, and customers were abandoning carts at alarming rates. The site felt clunky, navigation was confusing, and checkout was a nightmare on mobile.

  • Outdated visual design with poor visual hierarchy
  • Mobile experience: 34% of traffic, 8% of revenue
  • 5.2s average page load time (mobile)
  • 43% cart abandonment on mobile
  • Unclear product information and sizing guides
  • Checkout process required 6+ steps

Our Solution

Mobile-First Rebuild

Completely redesigned from the ground up with mobile as the primary focus. Touch-friendly navigation, optimized tap targets, and responsive image loading made mobile the star experience.

Streamlined Checkout

Reduced checkout steps from 6 to 2. One-page checkout, auto-detected shipping address, guest checkout option, and express payment methods increased conversion across all devices.

Visual Hierarchy & Trust

Modern design system with clear visual hierarchy. Product images 60% larger, prominent trust badges, customer reviews, size guides, and color swatches increased confidence and reduced returns.

Performance Optimization

Image optimization, lazy loading, code splitting, and CDN deployment reduced page load from 5.2s to 3.1s. 95/100 Lighthouse score achieved across pages.

Key Changes

Navigation
Cleaner & Mobile-Optimized
Before: Horizontal nav with 12+ links, dropdown menus, search was hard to find
After: Sticky header, mega-menu on desktop, hamburger on mobile, search always accessible
Product Pages
Large Images & Trust Signals
Before: Tiny product images, no size guide, minimal reviews, confusing options
After: 60% larger images, prominent reviews, size guide integrated, color swatches, trust badges
Checkout Flow
From 6 Steps to 2
Before: Cart page → Shipping → Billing → Payment → Review → Confirm = 43% abandonment
After: Shipping & Billing combined → Payment = 2-minute checkout, 12% abandonment
